Amtrak Forecast Report

Executive Summary

The models suggest that each business line should expect an increase in ridership in 2023 followed by significant drops in 2024 returning to ridership levels seen in 2022. The Other and Long Distance business lines saw the largest increases in ridership from 2022 to 2023 with 15.5% and 14.6% jumps respectively. The State Supported and Northeast Corridor business lines saw the largest decreases in ridership from 2023 to 2024 with -12.4% and -12.3% drops respectively.
